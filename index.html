Common Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Under ERC Resolution No. 09, Series of 2013 (implementing RA 9513), any qualified end-user with a renewable energy system up to 100 kWp can participate in net metering. Surplus electricity exported to the grid is credited at the prevailing generation charge. Credits accumulate monthly and are settled in cash annually. We handle the full interconnection application with your distribution utility.

Yes — all our panels are rated for 2,400 Pa wind load (equivalent to ~200 km/h winds). Our mounting systems are engineered to ASCE 7-22 standards and verified against local wind zone maps from PAGASA. We've had zero structural failures across 2,400+ installations including Typhoon Carina (2024) and previous strong typhoons.

Required permits include: Building Permit (BPLO), Electrical Permit (local government), Distribution Utility Interconnection Permit, and ERC Net Metering Application. SolarPro PH processes all permits on your behalf as part of the installation package. Timeline is typically 4–8 weeks depending on your distribution utility's processing speed.

Yes. Under RA 9513 Section 15, solar energy equipment is VAT-exempt. Additionally, income from net metering credits is not subject to income tax for residential users. Commercial and industrial installations may qualify for additional fiscal incentives under the CREATE Act through PEZA or BOI registration depending on project scale.

Physical installation typically takes 1–3 days for residential systems (3–10 kWp). The full timeline from signed contract to grid-connected system is usually 6–10 weeks, with most of that time spent on permit processing. We provide weekly status updates throughout the process.

We partner with BDO, BPI, and RCBC for solar-specific home improvement loans at preferential rates. SSS and Pag-IBIG members may also access solar financing through their respective home improvement loan programs. Typical loan terms range from 3–10 years. Many customers choose 5-year financing and still see positive cash flow from day one.
